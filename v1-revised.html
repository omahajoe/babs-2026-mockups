What Changed & Why

  • Hero repositioned: Now leads with "Real business gets done" — aspirational, not oppositional. BABS is framed as a movement, not a reaction.
  • New "Who This Room Is For" section replaces the old "What This Isn't" anti-positioning section. Leads with who belongs here instead of who doesn't.
  • "The Room Changes Everything" is now a prominent section header, framing the experience around transformation.
  • New "What You Leave With" section clearly articulates concrete attendee outcomes — relationships, plans, accountability, and deals.
  • "Not corporate, not woo-woo, not bro" kept as a single qualifier line in the hero — not a full section. It works as a filter without dominating the message.
  • Champions Code moved after "Who This Is For" — the room comes first, the code explains why it works.
